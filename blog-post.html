Sekai Beyond 2025

  • Event: Sekai Beyond
  • Location: Seattle, WA
  • Date: Saturday, October 11, 2025
  • Attendees: ~200

Sekai Beyond was an intimate convention experience in my home state of Washington. As a local artist, I had the opportunity to connect with fellow creators and fans in a more personal setting. The smaller scale allowed for meaningful conversations and direct feedback on my artwork.

I showcased my original illustrations and took custom commission requests. This event helped me build confidence in my pricing and presentation skills, setting a strong foundation for larger conventions.

IKKicon 2026

  • Event: IKKicon
  • Location: Austin, TX
  • Date: Friday, January 9 - Sunday, January 11, 2026
  • Attendees: 5,550

IKKicon in Austin was my first experience with a mid-sized convention. The vibrant Texas community and diverse attendee base created an exciting atmosphere for showcasing my work. I expanded my portfolio to include more varied styles and themes.

This event was particularly memorable for the collaborative spirit among artists. I participated in group activities and learned new techniques from fellow creators. The three-day format allowed for deeper engagement with fans and potential clients.

Anime Washington 2026

  • Event: Anime Washington
  • Location: Puyallup, WA
  • Date: Friday, February 6 - Sunday, February 8, 2026
  • Attendees: TBD

Returning to Washington for Anime Washington, I'm excited to participate in another local convention. Puyallup's proximity to home makes this event special - I can focus entirely on creating and connecting rather than worrying about travel logistics.

Having attended before, I know the community's enthusiasm and look forward to building on previous relationships. This event allows me to experiment with new display techniques and merchandise options tailored to the Pacific Northwest audience.

Anime Matsuri 2026

  • Event: Anime Matsuri
  • Location: Houston, TX
  • Date: Thursday, July 23 - Sunday, July 26, 2026
  • Attendees: 15,000

Anime Matsuri represents my largest convention experience to date. The four-day event in Houston brings together artists and fans from across the country, creating an electric atmosphere for creative expression and business opportunities.

I'm preparing for this event by expanding my inventory and developing special pieces for the occasion. The scale of Anime Matsuri will test my organizational skills and allow me to reach a broader audience with my artwork.
